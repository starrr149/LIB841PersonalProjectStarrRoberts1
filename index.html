¡Hola! Me llamo Srta. Roberts. Soy profesora de español uno de Doss High School. Or Hello! My name is Ms. Roberts I am the Spanish I teacher of Doss High School.

I am looking forward to the school year and that you are joining me on this journey in learning Spanish! Although this class is an elective, it is an ACADEMIC elective which helps prepare you for college success. Anticipate work! Anticipate learning to speak/write/read/listen in Spanish!

Some information about the class:
This course is designed to give students a basic understanding of Spanish language and culture. We will study vocabulary, verbs, and grammar to give students communication skills in the world language.
