Method of Instruction

Class will be conducted in Spanish and English. Students are expected to actively participate in discussions and projects. We will use a combination of approaches-lecture, discussion, and cooperative learning. We use games, partners, teams, memorization drills, role playing, small group/large group activities, multimedia lessons, Google Slide presentations, videos, and viewing DVDs for instruction.
